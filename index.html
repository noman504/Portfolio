About Me

My name is Noman Ibna Billal — a Computer Science Engineering student at Daffodil International University, passionate about web development, data analysis, and building meaningful digital products.

I have hands-on experience with a wide spectrum of technologies, from systems-level C/C++ to modern full-stack development with React and Spring Boot. Every project I take on sharpens both my technical depth and my problem-solving instincts.

Beyond the code, I'm a national debater — an arena that has given me an unusual gift: the ability to see every challenge from multiple angles and communicate ideas with clarity and conviction. I believe the best engineers are also great thinkers.

Teaching is the other side of me. Through my YouTube channel Concept Genix, I break down complex concepts so students can actually understand and enjoy them. My motto: "Learn, Explore, Lead."
