About Me

My name is Noman Ibna Billal — a Computer Science Engineering student at Daffodil International University, passionate about web development, data analysis, and building meaningful digital products.

I have hands-on experience with a wide spectrum of technologies, from systems-level C/C++ to modern full-stack development with React and Spring Boot. Every project I take on sharpens both my technical depth and my problem-solving instincts.
